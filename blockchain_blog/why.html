Why Blockchain?

Understanding the need for blockchain technology

Traditional Systems

  • Centralized control
  • Single point of failure
  • Data can be modified
  • Lower transparency

Blockchain-Based Systems

  • Decentralized network
  • No single point of failure
  • Immutable data
  • High transparency

Why Blockchain is Better

Blockchain eliminates the need for trust by using cryptographic security and decentralization. Once data is recorded, it cannot be changed without detection.

Use Case: Certificate Verification

Educational certificates require authenticity and trust. Blockchain ensures certificates cannot be forged or altered, making verification simple and reliable.

Conclusion

Blockchain is not just a technology for cryptocurrencies. It is a powerful solution for secure, transparent, and trustworthy digital systems.
